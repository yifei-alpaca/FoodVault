About


Overview

We are team of Singapore Management University (SMU) students trying to reduce food waste in Singapore.

Our main audience is the public and F&B stalls. According to statistics, F&B stalls waste large amount of food daily. By allowing F&B stalls to sign up with us, they would be able to donate surplus food to the public instead of disposing the extra food. This way, food wastage can be reduced while providing food aid to the wider public. Additionally, the platform is also open to the public to learn more about food wastage interactively and try out our recipes generator based on the leftover food in their home.

Mission

Slow down climate change and alleviate hunger, by providing a platform for F&Bs to donate surplus food to the public through distribution programs.

Goals

  • Mitigate Climate Change by reducing Food Wastage
  • Minimize Food Wastage for F&B Stalls locally
  • Providing free surplus food as means of charity
  • Generate Recipes based on leftover food
  • Educate the public on food wastage interactively and creatively


Lives and household impacted positively after using our platform

F&B Businesses impacted and improved food resource planning after using our platform.

Years of experience in the community service industry before official launch

Awards given by National Environment Agency (NEA) for making an impact

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Food Vault?

Food Vault is an responsive platform for F&B stalls to donate their excess food for the day, or any food items that are expiring soon and unused, to the public who require food aid.

How does Food Vault works?

The public can see and choose to come down to the venue if they require any food aid. Users can use utilise the website to generate feed recipes based on their leftovers, educate themselves on how they can contribute to reduce food wastage via posters and games.

Where do I find the merchants?

You can click on "Service" at the top of the Navigation bar and select "List of Merchants".

Do we need to register if we are collecting the food?

You do NOT need to register an account to collect the food. You can go straight down to the location without any hassle. However, upon collecting you can write a review on the merchant's page.

Im a F&B store owner. How do I know my company has helped in reducing food waste?

You can register with us and we would provide you an overview dashboard of how much have you been donating food and reducing food wastage.
