About

Overview

We are team of Singapore Management University (SMU) students trying to reduce food waste in Singapore.

Our main audience is the public and F&B stalls. According to statistics, F&B stalls waste large amount of food daily. By allowing F&B stalls to sign up with us, they would be able to donate surplus food to the public instead of disposing the extra food. This way, food wastage can be reduced while providing food aid to the wider public. Additionally, the platform is also open to the public to learn more about food wastage interactively and try out our recipes generator based on the leftover food in their home.

Learn More

Mission

Slow down climate change and alleviate hunger, by providing a platform for F&Bs to donate surplus food to the public through distribution programs.

Goals

  • Mitigate Climate Change by reducing Food Wastage
  • Minimize Food Wastage for F&B Stalls locally
  • Providing free surplus food as means of charity
  • Generate Recipes based on leftover food
  • Educate the public on food wastage interactively and creatively

F&B Businesses impacted and improved food resource planning after using our platform.
