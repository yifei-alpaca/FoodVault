About

Overview

We are team of Singapore Management University (SMU) students trying to save the earth by slowing down climate change. While there are many ways to do so, our team decided to reduce food waste while alleviating hunger to public.

Our main audience is the public and F&B stalls. According to statistics, F&B stalls waste large amount of food daily, from preparation of extra food daily to canned food or products reaching shelf life. By allowing F&B stalls to donate the surplus food products to the public, food wastage can be reduced while providing food aid, and free food, to the wider public.

Learn More

Mission

Slow down climate change and alleviate hunger, by providing a platform for F&Bs to donate surplus food to the public through distribution programs.

Goals

Our platform aims to :

  • Mitigate Climate Change by reducing Food Wastage
  • Minimize Food Wastage for F&B Stalls locally
  • Providing free surplus food as means of charity

Lives and household impacted positively after using our platform

F&B Businesses impacted and improved food resource planning after using our platform.

Years of experience in the community service industry before official launch

Awards given by National Environment Agency (NEA) for making an impact
